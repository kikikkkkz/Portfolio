About Me

I am a 3rd-year Interactive Art & Technology student at Simon Fraser University. My interest is fashion and technology and I want to pursue a career in making more possibilities between these two industries.
"Designer" As a fashion lover, I work as a freelance street style photographer during New York fashion week and go deep into the industry through reading lots of news and researching through the website and social media.
"Programmer"As a technologist, I learn coding not only in the class but also do lots of self-study during free time. By conducting these activities, I feel more determined towards myself and also my future career. I also worked on a project for mobile app.
"UX to me..."In terms of user experience, I like to think about design we see in everyday life. I always pay attention to the design failure and come up with multiple ways to improve it. How to make users feel delighted and make the user experience more fluent are the problems I want to focus on.
